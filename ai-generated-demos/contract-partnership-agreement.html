Clause 11
Exit Provisions and Dissolution

The remaining Partner shall have the first right of refusal to purchase the Exiting Partner's interest at a fair market value determined by an independent valuer agreed upon by both Partners. In the event of dissolution of the partnership, assets shall be liquidated, and proceeds distributed to creditors first, then to Partners according to their capital accounts and profit-sharing ratios. Specific provisions for death, disability, or material breach will be detailed in an annexure or separate addendum.
