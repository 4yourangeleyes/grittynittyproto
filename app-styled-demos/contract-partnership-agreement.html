THIS AGREEMENT is entered into on 4 December 2025

BETWEEN: Founder Enterprises ("Service Provider")

AND: Investor Partners ("Client")

INDUSTRY: Business Partnership

WHEREAS the parties wish to enter into an agreement for the provision of professional services as detailed herein.

Clause 1
Parties
This Partnership Agreement (the 'Agreement') is made and entered into on [Date of Agreement] by and between Founder Enterprises, represented by [Founder Partner Representative Name], (hereinafter referred to as 'Founder Partner') and Investor Partners, represented by [Investor Partner Representative Name], (hereinafter referred to as 'Investor Partner').
Clause 2
Purpose of Partnership
The Parties hereby agree to form a business partnership (the 'Partnership') for the purpose of operating a general professional services business and engaging in related activities, under the name of [Partnership Name, if applicable].
Clause 3
Capital Contribution
The partners agree to contribute capital to the Partnership in the following proportions: Founder Partner shall contribute sixty percent (60%) of the total initial capital, and Investor Partner shall contribute forty percent (40%) of the total initial capital. Specific contributions (monetary or in-kind) shall be detailed in Schedule A attached hereto, which forms an integral part of this Agreement.
Clause 4
Profit and Loss Sharing
Net profits and losses of the Partnership shall be allocated between the partners in the same proportion as their capital contributions: sixty percent (60%) to Founder Partner and forty percent (40%) to Investor Partner. Distributions of profits shall be made [e.g., 'quarterly' or 'annually'] or as otherwise agreed upon by the partners, provided that the Partnership maintains sufficient reserves for operational needs.
Clause 5
Decision-Making Authority
All ordinary business decisions shall be made by a simple majority vote of the partners. For the avoidance of doubt, Founder Partner holds 60% of the voting rights and Investor Partner holds 40% of the voting rights on ordinary matters. However, critical decisions, including but not limited to, significant capital expenditures exceeding R250,000, amendment of this Agreement, admission of new partners, dissolution of the Partnership, or sale of substantial assets, shall require the unanimous written consent of both partners.
Clause 6
Management and Responsibilities
The day-to-day management and operational responsibilities shall be primarily overseen by Founder Partner. Investor Partner shall provide strategic guidance and oversight, and contribute to key decision-making processes. Specific roles and responsibilities may be further detailed in an operational annex to this Agreement.
Clause 7
Term of Agreement
This Agreement shall commence on [Start Date of Partnership] and shall continue indefinitely unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions herein.
Clause 8
Exit Strategy / Dissolution
A partner may withdraw from the Partnership by providing not less than ninety (90) days' written notice to the other partner. In the event of withdrawal, death, or permanent disability of a partner, the remaining partner shall have the option to purchase the outgoing partner's interest at a fair market value, determined by an independent valuation process agreed upon by the partners. If the remaining partner does not exercise this option within [e.g., '60 days'], the Partnership shall be dissolved, and its assets liquidated in accordance with South African law.
Clause 9
Dispute Resolution
Any dispute or difference arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, including any question regarding its existence, validity or termination, shall first be referred to mediation. If the dispute is not resolved through mediation within thirty (30) days, it shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration in accordance with the rules of the Arbitration Foundation of Southern Africa (AFSA).
Clause 10
Liability
The partners acknowledge that, unless otherwise provided by law, they shall be jointly and severally liable for the debts and obligations of the Partnership. Each partner shall indemnify and hold harmless the other partner from any liabilities arising from their individual gross negligence or wilful misconduct.
Clause 11
Confidentiality
Each partner undertakes to keep confidential all information relating to the business affairs, finances, and trade secrets of the Partnership and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other partner, both during the term of the Partnership and indefinitely thereafter.
Clause 12
Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
Clause 13
Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and arrangements, whether oral or written, relating to the subject matter hereof.
Clause 14
Amendment
No amendment, variation, or consensual cancellation of this Agreement shall be of any force or effect unless reduced to writing and signed by both partners.
