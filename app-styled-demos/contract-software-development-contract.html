THIS AGREEMENT is entered into on 4 December 2025

BETWEEN: App Developers SA ("Service Provider")

AND: Innovation Inc ("Client")

INDUSTRY: Software Development

WHEREAS the parties wish to enter into an agreement for the provision of professional services as detailed herein.

Clause 1
Parties
This Service Agreement ('Agreement') is made and entered into between App Developers SA, a company duly registered in the Republic of South Africa (hereinafter 'the Service Provider'), and Innovation Inc, a company duly registered in the Republic of South Africa (hereinafter 'the Client').
Clause 2
Scope of Work
The Service Provider agrees to design, develop, test, and deploy a custom mobile application (hereinafter 'the App') for the Client. The App will be developed for [Specify Target Platforms, e.g., iOS and Android] platforms and will include functionalities as detailed in the attached Technical Specification Document (Annexure A), which forms an integral part of this Agreement. Any additional features, modifications, or deviations from Annexure A shall be subject to a separate change request process and may incur additional costs and timeline adjustments.
Clause 3
Timeline and Milestones
The development of the App shall be completed within an estimated period of 18 weeks from the Effective Date of this Agreement, subject to the Client's timely provision of all necessary information, content, and approvals. The project will adhere to the following key milestones:

1. Phase 1: Discovery & Planning (2 weeks) - Requirements gathering, technical specification finalisation.

2. Phase 2: UI/UX Design (4 weeks) - Wireframes, mockups, design approval.

3. Phase 3: Development (8 weeks) - Frontend and backend coding, API integration.

4. Phase 4: Testing & Quality Assurance (2 weeks) - Bug fixing, performance testing, client acceptance testing.

5. Phase 5: Deployment & Launch (2 weeks) - Submission to app stores, final client acceptance.
Clause 4
Payment Terms
The total project fee for the services rendered under this Agreement is R250,000.00 (Two Hundred and Fifty Thousand Rand), excluding Value Added Tax (VAT). VAT at 15% amounts to R37,500.00 (Thirty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred Rand). The Grand Total, inclusive of VAT, is R287,500.00 (Two Hundred and Eighty-Seven Thousand Five Hundred Rand). The payment schedule is as follows:

1. A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the Grand Total, amounting to R143,750.00, shall be due and payable by the Client upon the signing of this Agreement.

2. The remaining balance of 50% of the Grand Total, amounting to R143,750.00, shall be due and payable upon successful completion of the App development, final testing, and acceptance by the Client, prior to deployment to app stores.

All payments shall be made via Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) to the Service Provider's nominated bank account. Overdue invoices shall accrue interest at a rate of Prime + 2% per annum, compounded monthly.
Clause 5
Intellectual Property Rights
Upon full and final payment of all amounts due under this Agreement, all Intellectual Property Rights in the custom-developed software code, unique designs, and content created specifically for the App by the Service Provider shall transfer to the Client. The Service Provider retains ownership of any pre-existing tools, libraries, frameworks, or generic code components not exclusively developed for the Client, which may be incorporated into the App, granting the Client a perpetual, non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use such components solely as part of the App.
Clause 6
Confidentiality
Both parties agree to treat all information disclosed by the other party as confidential, including but not limited to business plans, technical data, trade secrets, and customer information. Neither party shall disclose such confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party, except as required by law. This obligation shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement.
Clause 7
Warranty
The Service Provider warrants that the App will function substantially in accordance with the specifications outlined in Annexure A for a period of three (3) months ('Warranty Period') from the date of final acceptance by the Client. During this Warranty Period, the Service Provider will rectify any material defects or bugs reported by the Client without additional charge. This warranty does not cover issues arising from third-party software, misuse, unauthorised modifications by the Client or third parties, or changes in the operating environment beyond the Service Provider's control.
Clause 8
Liability
The Service Provider's total liability to the Client for any and all claims arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, whether in contract, delict (including negligence), or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client to the Service Provider under this Agreement. The Service Provider shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, including loss of profits, data, or business interruption, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.
Clause 9
Cancellation Policy
In the event the Client wishes to cancel this Agreement prior to completion, the Client shall provide written notice to the Service Provider. The Client shall be liable for all work completed up to the date of cancellation, calculated at the Service Provider's standard hourly rates, plus any non-recoverable third-party costs incurred. Any deposit paid will be offset against these charges, and any remaining balance will be refunded or become immediately due, as applicable. If the Service Provider cancels the agreement, all unearned fees will be refunded, and the Service Provider will assist in the handover of work completed to date.
Clause 10
Support and Maintenance
Upon expiration of the Warranty Period, any further support, maintenance, or enhancements to the App will be provided under a separate Support and Maintenance Agreement, the terms and fees of which will be negotiated and agreed upon by both parties.
Clause 11
Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
Clause 12
Dispute Resolution
Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, including any question regarding its existence, validity or termination, shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration in accordance with the rules of the Arbitration Foundation of Southern Africa (AFSA), by an arbitrator or arbitrators appointed by AFSA.
Clause 13
Entire Agreement
This Agreement, including any annexures referred to herein, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written, between the parties.
Clause 14
Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable by any court of competent jurisdiction, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
